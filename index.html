WHY MUE?

There are thousands of cryptocurrency projects, with more added every single day. Of these thousands, only the platforms with the most robust blockchain security and stability, along with an established use case ecosystems will survive. MonetaryUnit aims at being a leading cryptocurrency by creating these use cases for not only MUE but other cryptocurrencies as well.

 

The blockchain space is plagued with "trying to put a square peg in a round hole". Many use cases for cryptocurrencies feel strained or ineffective. MonetaryUnit is focusing on use cases that allow those currently pioneering in the cryptocurrency space, and eventually the general public, to use their MUE and other cryptocurrencies to acquire goods and services.

 

We can't sit around and wait for new cryptocurrency market platforms to be populated by goods and services. We are instead targeting well established platforms to incorporate cryptocurrencies in a way that feels innate to the platform in the first place and not just an afterthought.

Instant Transactions

For urgent transactions a payment can be instantly received and confirmed by the recipient via the Instantsend function.

Self Sustaining

The economic design of MonetaryUnit means that 10% of all MUE generated by the blockchain is reserved for the benefit of the project ecosystem. The utilization of these funds is controlled through a decentralized community voting mechanism.

Commercial Arms & Affiliates

MUE is focused on the continual development of its surrounding ecosystem to ensure maximum currency exposure and usage which benefits the cryptocurrency community as a whole.

MasterNodes

Our unique business infrastructure ensures stability within the community and constant maintenance.

Community & MUE Team

Helpful community and project team always on hand to assist with questions and general blockchain education.

Long Term Low Inflation Rate

Inflation (MUE generated by the block mining process) is a low 3% per year. This promotes the use and distribution of MUE on a sustainable trajectory while the world adopts cryptocurrencies. 40 new MUE will be minted every 40 seconds for the next 130 years.

about us
